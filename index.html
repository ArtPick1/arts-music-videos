“Are You Living a Lie” presents a musically driven, lyrical reflection on spiritual identity, the emptiness of worldly pursuits, and the invitation to surrender fully to Christ. Not Just Another Pretty Voice

A call to shout in worship—TERUAH style. Loud, raw praise that breaks down walls (Psalm 100:1–2).
Click to read more…
This isn’t a soft ballad for Sunday morning. It’s a declaration that worship is war. Based on Psalm 100:1–2 and the Hebrew concept of TERUAH — the battle cry, the trumpet blast, the shout that brought Jericho's walls down — this song calls believers to worship with power and purpose. It rebukes the idol of polished, performance-based worship and reminds us that God is looking for surrendered, Spirit-filled praise that shakes the atmosphere. Whether your voice is smooth or rough, the command is the same: shout to the Lord with joy, lift up a sound that tears through spiritual strongholds, and don’t hold back.
